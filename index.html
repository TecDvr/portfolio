SLACKTIDE QUIZ APP

Diving the vast oceans our planet holds can be a relaxing and rewarding pastime, as long as you have slack tides. Slack tide is time between high and low tidal exchanges that gives you the calm waters you seek. My quiz app will help you gain the knowledge necessary for finding these “slacks” and allow you to have safe, enjoyable dives for years to come. So dive in, its slack tide!
